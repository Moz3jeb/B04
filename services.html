Our Services

We offer a range of AI-powered tools and services designed to help organizations become more sustainable and environmentally responsible.

Carbon Footprint Tracker

A dashboard that uses machine learning to monitor and calculate your company's carbon emissions in real time. It pulls data from energy usage, supply chains, and transportation to show where emissions come from and suggests ways to reduce them.

Smart Energy Management

Our AI system analyzes building energy patterns and automatically adjusts heating, cooling, and lighting to cut waste. It learns from daily usage and weather data to optimize energy consumption. Most clients see a 20-30% drop in energy bills.

Waste Reduction Analytics

Uses computer vision and data analysis to help manufacturing and food companies track waste. The system identifies patterns in waste generation and recommends changes to processes that can significantly reduce waste output.

Green Supply Chain Optimization

We analyze logistics data to find more efficient routes, suppliers, and packaging options. The AI considers both cost and environmental impact to find the best balance for your supply chain.

Environmental Impact Reports

Our platform automatically generates detailed environmental impact reports that meet international standards like GRI, CDP, and TCFD. Instead of weeks of manual data collection, get accurate reports in hours.

Sustainability Consulting AI

An AI-powered advisory tool that answers sustainability questions, helps with compliance, and gives personalized recommendations based on your industry and company size. Like having a sustainability expert available 24/7.
