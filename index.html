About Us

EcoMind AI is a technology startup that combines artificial intelligence with environmental sustainability. We started in 2024 with a simple idea — use AI to help businesses and communities reduce their environmental impact.

Our team of engineers, data scientists, and environmental experts works together to build tools that make going green easier and more practical for everyone. We believe technology should serve the planet, not just profit.

Our Mission

To make sustainable practices accessible to every organization through intelligent, easy-to-use technology solutions.
